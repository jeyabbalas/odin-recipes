Park and Ride One-Pan Chicken Pilaf

Description

Ingredients (serves 2 buffoons):

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion peeled and chopped
  • 1 diced skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tsp curry powder or paste
  • 1/3 cup of basmati rice
  • 2/3 cup of chicken stock
  • 1 cup of frozen mixed vegetables

Steps

  1. Heat the oil on a pan, then fry the onion until soft.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and fry for a further couple of minutes just to color the outside.
  3. Stir in the curry powder or paste, and rice. Cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in the chicken stock and stir in any larger bits of frozen vegetables.
  5. Bring to the boil, lower the heat, then cover the pan with a lid.
  6. Cook for ten minutes, then stir in the remaining vegetables, and the dates. Cook until the stock is absorbed.
  7. Serve.
