Not now Madam Venison, Cabbage, and Chestnut

Description

Ingredients (serves 2 simpletons):

  • 1 Savoy cabbage
  • Nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 x 125g/4oz lean venison steaks
  • 125g/4oz vacuum-packed chestnuts, roughly diced
  • One slug of red wine

Steps

  1. Remove the outer leaves and core of the cabbage and slice thinly.
  2. Place in a saucepan with 1cm of water and a good pinch of grated nutmeg.
  3. Boil/steam for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Heat the oil in a frying pan.
  5. Season the steaks and fry with the chestnuts for 2-3 minutes each side (or in accordance to your preference of done-ness).
  6. Drain the cabbage and add to the frying pan with a slug of red wine.
  7. Cook off the wine and serve.
