Slippery Wilf's Cauliflower Base Pizza

Description

Ingredients (serves 2 laughingstocksmen):

  • 500g/1lb cauliflower rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tomato purée
  • Topping of choice

Steps

  1. Place the cauliflower rice in a bowl and microwave on full power for three minutes.
  2. Pour the rice onto a tea towel and fold the towel around the rice.
  3. Twist the towel with all your might to force all the moisture out of the rice. Warning: you may look magnificent during this process.
  4. Put the post-squeezed rice back into a bowl and add the egg, Parmesan, and seasonings.
  5. Using your beautiful hands, spread the mixture into a pleasing, potentially circular, shape on a sheet of greaseproof paper.
  6. Place in a hot (200°C/392°F) oven for 5-6 minutes until it just turns golden.
  7. Remove from the oven and add toppings of your choice.
