Bubbles McParty's Sardine and Feta Cheese Wrap

Description

Ingredients (serves 2 Herberts):

  • 1 pita bread
  • 1 tin of sardines in lemon oil
  • 50g/2oz feta cheese
  • Tomato chopped
  • Lettuce chopped
  • Dates pitted
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Open up the pita bread and thrust the remaining ingredients into it with partial fury but a hint of love and care.
  2. Serve.
