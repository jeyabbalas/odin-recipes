Boots McFoolish Breakfast Porridge

Description

Ingredients (serves 2 halfwits):

  • 300ml/8 fl oz semi-skimmed milk
  • 300ml/8 fl oz water
  • 175g/6oz rolled oates
  • Salt to taste
  • A few tinned prunes
  • A handful tinned pears
  • A handful tinned plums
  • Brown sugar or chia seeds (if you're from London)

Steps

  1. Put the milk, water, oates, and sal into a saucepan. Stir and bring to boil.
  2. Reduce the heat to a simmer for 7-8 minutes. The more you stir the porridge now, the creamier it will be.
  3. When cooked, remove from heat and fold in the fruits.
  4. Serve in a bowl and sprinkle brown sugar on top. If you are from London: feel free to use chia seeds or mouse whiskers üê≠.
